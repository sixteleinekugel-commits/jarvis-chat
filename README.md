# Nova AI 618 

I built **Nova AI 618** from scratch at home with one goal in mind:
create a **powerful, fast, and completely free AI** that anyone can use instantly.

Inspired by **TON 618**, the largest black hole ever observed, Nova AI 618 represents **power, scale, and limitless potential** — just like the AI behind it.

Today, Nova AI 618 is more than just a chatbot — it's a **complete AI assistant**.

##  What Nova AI 618 can do

*  **Generate text** using advanced models (up to 70B parameters)
*  **Create images without limits**
*  **Analyze images** and understand visual content
*  **Voice conversation mode** — talk to the AI naturally, in real time
*  **Guest access** — no account, no friction, just use it

## ⚡ Why it's different

I didn’t want to build “just another AI tool”.
Nova AI 618 is designed to be:

* **Fast** (powered by optimized APIs like Groq)
* **Accessible** (free and available instantly)
* **Versatile** (text, images, voice — all in one place)

## ⚙️ Tech behind it

* Built with **LLaMA-based models and Groq APIs**
* Backend deployed on **Render**
* Optimized for speed and real-time interaction

## 🌐 Visibility

Nova AI 618 is **indexed on Google**, making it easily accessible to anyone searching for it online.
